Marocchino Coffee

If you love coffee and chocolate, you must try this Marocchino recipe! It's a delicious Italian coffee with layers of espresso, frothy milk, and a touch of chocolate sweetness in every sip!

Ingredients

  • 1 heaped teaspoon chocolate spread or chocolate hazelnut spread
  • 1 espresso coffee shot
  • â…“ cup milk of choice
  • Cocoa or cacao powder

Steps

  1. Take a spoonful of the chocolate spread and spread it along the inner surface of the glass using the back of the spoon
  2. Add the shot of espresso coffee into the glass
  3. Heat the milk either over the stovetop or in the microwave in a small pitcher.
  4. Froth the milk with a milk frother and pour over the espresso with a foamy layer on the top
  5. Dust with cacao powder using a fine mesh sieve for the final touch and enjoy your Marocchino Coffee!
