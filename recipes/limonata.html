Limonata

This traditional Italian Limonata is the most delicious, fragrant and flavorful lemonade I have ever tried. Super easy to prepare, I guarantee, try it once and you’ll be making it all summer long!

Ingredients

  • 4 organic lemons
  • 8 tablespoon sugar
  • Handful fresh mint leaven or basil leaves (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash lemons under cold running water and scrub them gently with a brush to remove any impurities.
  2. Zest all lemons using a microplane zester. Grate only the yellow part of the skin and avoid grating the white pith which will make lemonade bitter.
  3. Add granulated sugar and torn mint leaves. Mix everything well applying pressing and squeezing motion to extract the most flavor.
  4. Let sit 30 minutes or more (macerate).
  5. Squeeze lemon juice from zested lemons and add to sugar and zest mix. Stir well. Strain the mix using a strainer.
  6. When ready to serve, fill half of a glass with ice, add about ⅓ of a glass lemonade mix and top everything with soda water.
